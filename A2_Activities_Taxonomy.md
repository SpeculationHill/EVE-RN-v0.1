# A2_Activities_Taxonomy

## Purpose
Create a taxonomy of in‑game activities to categorize gameplay experiences and link them to tools and communities.

## Activity Categories
1. **Combat**:
   - *Mission Running*: PvE missions against NPCs for ISK rewards.  
   - *Ratting*: Clearing NPC pirate sites in asteroid belts and anomalies.  
   - *Incursions & Invasions*: Fighting Sansha Nation, Triglavian and other incursions.  
   - *Faction Warfare*: Player‑driven warzone between the four empires; involves capturing complexes and system control.  
   - *Null‑Sec PvP*: Sovereignty warfare, fleet battles, roaming.

2. **Industry**:
   - *Mining*: Harvesting ore, ice and gas in high, low, null or wormhole space.  
   - *Manufacturing*: Building ships, modules, ammunition and rigs from blueprints.  
   - *Planetary Industry*: Extracting and processing resources on planets.  
   - *Research & Invention*: Improving blueprint efficiency and inventing Tech II.  

3. **Logistics & Trade**:
   - *Hauling*: Moving goods between systems.  
   - *Market Trading*: Buying and selling items on player markets; paying taxes and broker fees (an ISK sink)【940571151247159†L83-L101】.  
   - *Contracts & Courier*: Accepting or creating courier contracts.

4. **Exploration**:
   - *Wormhole Exploration*: Scanning for wormholes which are temporary connections between systems【647673664077612†L154-L162】.  
   - *Cosmic Signatures*: Probing down relic/data sites, combat sites, gas sites.  

5. **Social & Community**:
   - *Public Fleets / NPSI*: Joining open fleets that engage any non‑blue target【976944329353990†L113-L117】.  
   - *Roleplay & Lore*: Participating in story-driven events and roleplay groups.  

## Notes
- Activities may overlap; for instance, wormhole ratting is both exploration and combat.
- Tag each activity with recommended skill levels and risk profiles (e.g., high‑sec mining is low risk; null‑sec ratting is high risk).
